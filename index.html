Open Geodag 2026 · May 12

A Cloud Native Geo Upgrade for Spatial Data Infrastructure

Chris Holmes
An exploration of how cloud-native geospatial formats — GeoParquet, COG, PMTiles, STAC, and more — can fundamentally upgrade how Spatial Data Infrastructures work. Featuring Portolan, a new open-source framework for building serverless SDIs that are AI-first, easy to implement, infinitely scalable, low cost, and fully sovereign. Includes live demonstrations with Dutch government data and AI-powered spatial analysis.
